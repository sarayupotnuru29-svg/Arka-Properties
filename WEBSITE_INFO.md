# Arka Property - Real Estate Website

A modern, premium real estate website built with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS.

## Pages

1. **Home** - Hero section, featured projects, about preview, why choose us, stats
2. **About** - Company story, mission/vision, values, trust factors
3. **Projects** - All 3 projects with detailed information and features
4. **Contact** - Contact information, business hours, Google Maps
5. **Enquiry** - Form with WhatsApp integration

## Projects Featured

1. **Sree City Kubera** - Premium residential project
2. **Sree City Vaibhav** - Modern architecture for families
3. **Sowbhagya Neppalle** - Serene community living
